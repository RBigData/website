Stable

We maintain stable distributions of all of our packages on the Comprehensive R Archive Network (CRAN). For information about installing these packages, see our comprehensive set of installation instructions.

Additionally, you can find current binaries of our packages for the Windows operating system at our sister site, HPSC.

Development

You can find development branches for all of our packages at our github. For information about installing our packages from github, see our comprehensive set of installation instructions.

For really new/under development (possibly broken!) versions of our packages, you can also check out our developer github accounts: snoweye and wrathematics.
